# Tincraft Reborn X

Tincraft Classic X is a Forge 1.12.2 version of the Tincraft series. It brings content from both Tincraft and Tincraft 2 to old versions of the game.

## Features

- Content from Tincraft and Tincraft 2
- New blocks and items
- Block entities
- NeoForge support
- Additional improvements and new content

## Why download Tincraft Reborn?

Tincraft Reborn continues the Tincraft experience on old Minecraft versions with Forge support and ongoing development.

## Important Information

- NeoForge is required.
- Tincraft Reborn is based on the Tincraft series and is developed by the original author.
- Some internal systems may differ from previous Tincraft releases.
- Tincore may not be required, depending on the version.

## Changes from Tincraft 2

- NeoForge-only
- May not use Tincore
- Updated for modern Minecraft versions
- Ongoing development and improvements
